About FlaskBoost

FlaskBoost is a web application designed to predict flight prices using advanced machine learning algorithms. Our goal is to provide users with accurate and reliable flight price predictions to help them make informed travel decisions.

Developed by a team of passionate developers and data scientists, FlaskBoost leverages the power of Flask for web development and integrates machine learning models to analyze various factors influencing flight prices.

Whether you're a frequent traveler or planning your next vacation, FlaskBoost aims to be your go-to tool for understanding and predicting flight costs.
